Dublin House Prices

The Property Price Register publishes the prices of all properties sold in Ireland going back to 2010. I've taken data for houses sales in Dublin and broke them up like a bar of chocolate to see what we can figure out about the city and how it's expanding. Some post code data was missing but it didn't take Naive Bayes Regression long to track it down. Check it out.

Positions in Basketball

Classically, there are three positions on a basketball team - two guards, two forward and a center. But some basketball stats fiends think this is wrong - the say a team is really a point guard, two wings and two forwards. Which theory is correct, or could they both be wrong and a third truth be out there? Check out this analysis of NBA stats using K-Means Clustering to find out.

What's Going on with Twitter?

I used the #ladyball hashtag used as part of Lidl's sponsorship of Ladies' GAA to ask: just what exactly do we mean by Twitter 'reach'? This was presented as a talk to Python Ireland in Spring of 2016.
